ESG — environmental, social, and governance practice — is shifting from a
reporting chore to something buyers, lenders, and partners increasingly expect.
For Bangladeshi corporates, the win is starting deliberately rather than
performing it.

## Begin with what's material

Not every ESG topic matters equally to every business. Identify the few that are
material to your sector and stakeholders, and focus there first. A short,
honest list beats a glossy report nobody believes.

## Governance is the foundation

The "G" is where credibility is built: clear controls, honest disclosure, and
decisions that hold up to scrutiny. Strong governance makes the environmental and
social work believable.

## Make it operational

ESG that lives only in a document doesn't last. Tie it to real processes, owners,
and a review cadence — the same discipline good operations already use.
